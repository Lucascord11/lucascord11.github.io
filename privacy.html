Privacy Policy for LastPlay

Last updated: January 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LastPlay collects, uses, and shares information.

Information We Collect

  • Camera and Video: LastPlay accesses your device camera to record video footage in a circular buffer. Videos are stored locally on your device.
  • Microphone: LastPlay accesses your microphone to record audio with videos.
  • Device Information: LastPlay uses device identifiers to connect your iPhone with your paired Apple Watch.

How We Use Information

  • All video and audio recordings are stored locally on your iPhone
  • Device identifiers are used only to establish connection between iPhone and Apple Watch
  • We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers
  • We do NOT share any information with third parties

Data Storage

  • All data remains on your device
  • Videos can be deleted at any time from within the app
  • No cloud storage or external transmission occurs
